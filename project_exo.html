Exoskeletal Arm & Neural Interface

/// OVERVIEW

Human Augmentation

The Exoskeletal Arm project aims to amplify human strength and endurance for industrial applications. By utilizing non-invasive Electromyography (EMG) sensors, the suit predicts user intent and actuates motors with less than 10ms of latency.

Current prototypes are fabricated from 7075 Aerospace Aluminum, providing a lightweight structure capable of lifting loads in excess of 50kg.

Key Features

  • Signal processing via custom PCB neural network.
  • Haptic feedback loops for grip strength modulation.
  • Modular battery packs for 4-hour operational uptime.

System Specs

MAX LOAD50 KG
ACTUATIONLinear Hydraulic
MATERIALAlu 7075-T6
